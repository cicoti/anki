The hills across the valley of the Ebro were long and white.
As colinas do outro lado do vale do Ebro eram longas e brancas.  

On this side there was no shade and no trees
Deste lado não havia sombras nem arvores

and the station was between two lines of rails in the sun.
e a estação ficava entre duas linhas de trilhos no sol.

Close against the side of the station
Perto da lateral da estação

there was the warm shadow of the building
havia a sombra quente do predio

and a curtain, made of strings of bamboo beads,
e uma cotina, feita de cordões de contas de bambu 

hung across the open door into the bar, to keep out flies.
perndurada na porta aberta para o bar, para manter as moscas do lado de fora.

The American and the girl with him sat at a table in the shade, outside the building.
O americano e a garota sentaram na mesa na sombra, fora do predio.

It was very hot and the express from Barcelona would come in forty minutes.
Estava muito quente e o expresso de Barcelona chegaria em quarenta minutos.

It stopped at this junction for two minutes and went on to Madrid.
Ele parou neste cruzamento por dois miutos e foi para Madrid.

"What should we drink?" the girl asked.
"O que devemos beber?" Perguntou a garota.

She had taken off her hat and put it on the table.
Ela tinha tirado o seu chapeu e o colocou sobre a mesa.

"It's pretty hot," the man said.
"Está muito quente", o homem disse.

"Let's drink beer."
"Vamos beber cerveja."

"Dos cervezas," the man said into the curtain.
"Dos cervezas," o homem disse para dentro da cortina.

"Big ones?" a woman asked from the doorway.
"Grandes?" a mulher perguntou do vão da porta.

"Yes. Two big ones."
"Sim. Duas grandes."
